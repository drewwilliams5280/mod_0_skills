class Food

attributes:
ingredients(array of strings)
spicy(boolean)
calories(integer)
prep_difficulty(array of integers)

methods:
add_ingredient (will add ingredients array)
is_spicy (will change spicy attribute to true if jalapenos are in ingredients)
update_calories (will increase or lower calories attribute depending on ingredients)
increase_difficulty (will increase difficulty of prep depending on number of ingredients in array)
